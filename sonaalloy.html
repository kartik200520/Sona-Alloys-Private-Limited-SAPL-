About Us

SAPL is a state-of-the-art integrated steel plant spread over 175 acres at MIDC Lonand, Pune-Satara Highway. We produce Pig Iron (0.334 MTPA installed capacity) and are expanding to value-added steel products. Our Blast Furnace features Bell Less Top Charging, Pulverised Coal Injection, and carbon refractory for high efficiency. The Sinter Plant enhances productivity while reducing pollution. With a 5 MW Waste Heat Recovery Captive Power Plant, we demonstrate commitment to sustainability. SAPL adheres to strict quality standards and holds ISO 9001:2015 certification.

Promoter: Shri Sonaram Jain, with decades of experience in metals & mining. Vision: To be a preferred supplier of quality steel products in Western India.

Our customer base includes major auto ancillary units in Pune (Chakan, Talegaon), construction companies in Mumbai, and engineering clusters in Gujarat. We also export pig iron to Middle East markets.

Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 (Quality), ISO 14001 (Environment) underway, and all products comply with BIS standards.

Manufacturing Facilities

The mini integrated steel plant comprises: (capacity & technology)

  • Raw Material Handling (RMHS) – 600 TPH
  • Sinter Plant (24 m²) – 600 TPD
  • Blast Furnace (240 m³) with PCI
  • Steel Melt Shop (2x30 T EAF, CCM)
  • Rolling Mill (0.2 MTPA) – TMT bars
  • Air Separation Unit (120 TPD O₂)
  • Captive Power Plant (5 MW WHR)
  • Water Reservoir & Recycling
  • Slag Grinding Unit (GGBFS) – 150 TPD
  • Continuous Casting Machine (4-strand)
  • 132 kV Substation & DG backup
  • In-house metallurgical lab
  • Ladle Furnace & Vacuum Degasser (for alloy steel)
  • Automatic Stacker & Reclaimer

⚙️ We use advanced process control (Level 2 automation) for consistent quality and low energy consumption. Our melt shop is equipped with ladle furnace and vacuum degassing for alloy steel. The Blast Furnace has a Campbell melter and gas recovery system.

Key production stats: 1400 TPD Hot Metal, 1200 TPD Crude Steel, 1000 TPD Rolled Products. Energy consumption: 5.8 Gcal/tcs (below industry average).
